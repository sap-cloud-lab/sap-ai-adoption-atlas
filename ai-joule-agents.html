Four concepts to keep separate

The entry point, the bounded operation, the goal-driven workflow, and the customer extension surface are not interchangeable.

Joule

The conversational entry point for supported information, navigation, skills, and agents.

Skills

Bounded operations for a known intent and predictable execution path.

Agents

Goal-driven work using context, reasoning, tools, controlled actions, and handoffs.

Joule Studio

The separate design-time build surface for governed customer-specific skills and agents; it is not an agent classification. Open Build & Technology.

Every blueprint uses the same control boundary

Agent-specific pages replace the backend product and process content while retaining identity, least privilege, human approval, monitoring, and evidence controls.

Identity

Resolve user, role, tenant, and destination before the agent accesses SAP data or operations.

Authority

Separate retrieval, proposal, draft creation, approval, and final execution permissions.

Evidence

Retain source context, tool calls, decisions, approvals, handoffs, and outcomes for review.
