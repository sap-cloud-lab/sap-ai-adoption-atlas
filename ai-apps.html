Embedded AI Features

SAP-delivered AI placed directly inside the business application and workflow where people already work.

What is embedded AI?

An AI capability delivered within an SAP product experience, using that application's business context, authorisations, data, and process controls. Examples include summaries, recommendations, matching, forecasting, natural-language search, and governed draft creation.

What is an embedded agent?

An agentic capability surfaced in an SAP application that can reason across multiple steps and propose or perform supported actions under SAP roles, approvals, and audit controls. An embedded agent is one form of embedded AI; most records in this catalogue are features, not agents.

Edition evidence rule: Public Cloud or Private Cloud is marked as confirmed only when the SAP source record explicitly names that S/4HANA Cloud edition. Capabilities delivered in another SAP product are labelled as a separate product, and ambiguous S/4HANA records remain marked for validation.
