Adoption & Governance

A repeatable customer method for edition validation, readiness, controlled pilots, production approval, evidence, and measurable business outcomes.

Keep the deployment paths separate

Use the same evaluation model for business value and risk, but verify availability and setup independently for each SAP cloud edition.

Public Cloud adoption path

  • Identify the supported SAP app, IAM app, business catalog, and user role.
  • Confirm tenant release, feature activation, region, and commercial entitlement.
  • Pilot with representative data and a small role-based user group.
  • Measure outcome, review generated content, and document operational ownership.

Private Cloud adoption path

  • Confirm installed release, feature package, and SAP Help support scope.
  • Validate embedded setup, BTP services, connectivity, identity, and authorizations.
  • Test the full technical path in a non-production landscape.
  • Plan lifecycle, upgrades, monitoring, support, and commercial consumption.

Governance gates

Every capability should have an accountable business owner and a clear control boundary before production use.

Data

Classify source data, retention, grounding, residency, privacy, and downstream reuse.

Authority

Define who can retrieve, propose, draft, approve, execute, and administer the capability.

Decision

Set human-review thresholds, prohibited uses, fallback behavior, and escalation routes.

Operation

Monitor quality, cost, model or release change, incidents, support ownership, and business value.

Customer readiness questions

These questions determine whether to consume standard SAP AI, configure an agent, build on BTP, or stop and resolve a prerequisite.

Business outcome

What decision, delay, manual task, or control weakness should change, and how will value be measured?

SAP landscape

Which product, edition, tenant release, region, apps, roles, BTP account, and connected systems are involved?

Commercial path

Is it base entitlement, premium AI, AI Units, another subscription, or still subject to tenant validation?
